BRIEF OF BURHANI COLLEGE:

About Burhani College: Established in 2001, Burhani College is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and offers undergraduate courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce.
The college is known for its commitment to providing quality education and holistic development opportunities to its students. Located in the heart of Mumbai, Burhani College has a well-equipped campus with modern facilities, including classrooms, laboratories, a library, and sports facilities.
The college has a dedicated faculty who are experts in their respective fields and are committed to providing a supportive learning environment for students. Over the years, Burhani College has earned a reputation for academic excellence and has produced many successful graduates who have excelled in their chosen fields.

FOUNDER OF BURHANI INSTITUE
Burhani College, located in Mumbai, India, was founded by the Anjuman-I-Islam in 1876. The Anjuman-I-Islam is one of the oldest Muslim educational organizations in India, established with the aim of promoting education among the Muslim community.
The college was founded with the vision of providing quality education to students from all sections of society, with a focus on academic excellence and character development. Over the years, Burhani College has grown to become one of the premier educational institutions in Mumbai, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in arts, science, and commerce.
